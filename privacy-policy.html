Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12/30/2024


Sciology Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.sciologycamp.org (the "Website").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information
Personal Information:
Name
Email address
We do not collect or process transactions, nor do we collect information such as your operating system or IP address.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
To provide and maintain the Website
To communicate with you about updates, promotions, or support
To comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
Access to your personal data
Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
Deletion of your personal data
Restriction of data processing
Objection to data processing

Data portability

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@sciologycamp.org

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take additional measures to protect the privacy of children. We collect personal information (name and email address) solely for the purposes of providing services, communicating updates, or responding to inquiries. Parents or guardians may contact us at info@sciologycamp.org to review, update, or delete their child’s information.

Third-Party Links

The Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before submitting your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
